Classpublic class Feed

Contains the methods for the Feed method group in the Facebook API. Even though the events are listed here, they're really broadcast from the FacebookService instance itself to make using the service easier.

Publishes a Mini-Feed story to the user corresponding to the session_key parameter, and publishes News Feed stories to the friends of that user.

Parameters

body:String (default = "") — (Optional) The markup displayed in the feed story's body section.
 
image_1:String (default = "") — (Optional) The URL of an image to be displayed in the News Feed story.
 
image_1_link:String (default = "") — (Optional) The URL destination after a click on the image referenced by image_1.
 
image_2:String (default = "") — (Optional) The URL of an image to be displayed in the News Feed story.

image_3_link:String (default = "") — (Optional) The URL destination after a click on the image referenced by image_3.
 
image_4:String (default = "") — (Optional) The URL of an image to be displayed in the News Feed story.
